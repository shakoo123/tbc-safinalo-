Part i — heraldic silver cross at the head of the emerald field, below it a silver rock (Katsakhi column), on top of which a golden seat is erected. Part II — Three gold ronodas ("wagons") in the Sevad Valley, above them a miner's silver shovel. In the lower part of the coat-of-arms, both halves are crossed by three reduced bands of silver. The silver crown of the city is placed above the shield of the coat of arms - a three-towered rampart (castle-fortress-shaped), with a half-open gate. Below the shield is placed the silver-emerald ribbon of the coat of arms, on the silver field of which the motto - "Chiathura" is written in black

Description

The flag of Chiaturi municipality is the state symbol of Chiaturi municipality together with the coat of arms. Accepted by the City Council of Chiaturi MunicipalityFebruary 5, 2016S The flag is divided horizontally into two equal parts. The left part represents the green field, and the right part represents the azure field. In the upper left corner of the green field of the flag, the Bolnur-Katzkhuri cross is depicted. The proportions of the flag are 2:3

Chiatura is located in the narrow valley of the Chiaturi Plateau and the Kvirili River, 340-500 meters above sea level, on the Gomi-Sachkhere-Zestafoni highway. 220 km from Tbilisi (by railway). Chiatura has a fairly humid maritime subtropical climate, with moderately cold winters and relatively dry, hot summers. Average air temperature is +14 °C, January +2.4 °C, July +23.1 °C, absolute minimum -20 °C, absolute maximum +42 °C, precipitation 1100 mm. per year. The central settlement of the city is located on both banks of the Kvirili River and is surrounded by high, steep cliffs on almost all sides. Industrial areas are adjacent to it with a radius of 2-6 km
